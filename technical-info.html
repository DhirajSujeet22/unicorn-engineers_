Technical Information About Mechanical Seal

  1. Home
  2. Technical info

Mechanical Seal is a device used to stop 100% leakage from Rotary Equipment. It has Rotating & Stationary faces with Elastomers & energize component like spring or Bellow. It has several

Advantages:

1. Stop 100% Leakage

2. Save Energy

3. Reduce Maintenance

4. Reduce environment Hazards

5. Improve Work Culture

6. Keep Clean Work Station

7. Reduce Shaft / Sleeve erosion

8. Save Manpower

Important Factors in selection of Mechanical Seals:

1. Pressure / Vacuum

2. Temperature

3. Speed

4. Corrosion

5. PH Value

6. Nature of liquids
