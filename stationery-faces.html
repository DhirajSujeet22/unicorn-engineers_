Stationary Faces

It is vital part of Mechanical Seal remain static in housing. It is non-moving part hence gives good life once fix properly. Stationary faces are available in different shapes like D, L, T, H, Du eTungsten Carbide. Stationary face fits in housing with Elastomer in various shapes to stop leakage. It can be designed in any other shape also suiting to application.
